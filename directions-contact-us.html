Getting to the Infant and Child Language Centre:

By Car/Taxi

Via Ayer Rajah Expressway (AYE)

  • Take exit 9 to Clementi Road
  • Turn left into Kent Ridge Crescent from Entrance B
  • Turn right into Kent Ridge Drive at traffic junction
  • For DROP OFF ONLY, turn into Carpark 14 and stop at AS5. (Please note that there is NO visitor's parking allowed at Carpark 14)
  • For PARKING, go straight, past Carpark 14 and continue straight
  • Turn right into Carpark 15, before walking to AS5
  • Take the lift to level 4, we are at 04-30

Via Pasir Panjang Road

  • Turn in at Heng Mui Keng Terrace
  • Turn left into Kent Ridge Drive
  • For DROP OFF ONLY, go past Carpark 15 and turn right into Carpark 14, stopping at AS5. (Please note that there is NO visitor's parking allowed at Carpark 14)
  • For PARKING, look out for entrance to Carpark 15 the left and turn in
  • Walk towards the AS5 lift and take to level 4, we are at 04-30

Parking charges

  • Get a complimentary parking ticket from us
  • Remember to remove your cash card from your IU before exiting the carpark – no additional compensation can be provided if you forget!

By Public Transport

From Kent Ridge MRT Station

  • Board NUS internal shuttle Bus service A1 from Kent Ridge Bus Stop (bus stop number 18331)
  • Alight 7 stops later at AS7 along Kent Ridge Drive and you will be able to see AS5 building just behind the bus stop
  • Walk towards the junction of Kent Ridge Drive and Computing Drive
  • Turn left into Carpark 14, be careful of oncoming vehicles
  • Walk towards the AS5 lift and take to level 4, we are at 04-30
  • Walking distance: Approximately 150m

From Clementi MRT Station

  • Take bus service number 96 from the bus interchange (opposite Clementi MRT station)
  • Bus 96 turns into NUS from Entrance B
  • Alight at the 1st bus stop (bus stop number 16199) after it turns
  • Cross at the traffic light junction towards Kent Ridge Drive
  • Walk and turn left into Carpark 14
  • Walk past the Shaw Foundation Building AS7, until you see AS5
  • Walk towards the AS5 lift and take to level 4, we are at 04-30
  • Walking distance: Approximately 500m

From Buona Vista MRT Station

  • Cross via the overhead bridge towards the bus stop opposite Buona Vista MRT station near the Ministry of Education building
  • Take bus service number 95 from the bus stop
  • Alight 3 stops later at the National University Hospital (bus stop number 18221)
  • Take internal shuttle bus service A1
  • Alight 7 stops later at AS7 along Kent Ridge Drive and you will be able to see AS5 building just behind the bus stop
  • Walk towards the junction of Kent Ridge Drive and Computing Drive
  • Turn left into Carpark 14, be careful of oncoming vehicles
  • Walk towards the AS5 lift and take to level 4, we are at 04-30
  • Walking distance: Approximately 150m

From Harbourfront MRT Station

  • Take bus service numbers 10, 30 or 143 from the bus stop outside Vivocity or Harbourfront MRT station
  • Alight at the last stop of the service at Kent Ridge Terminal
  • Walk up the staircase and you will be able to see the Shaw Foundation Building AS7 just across the road
  • Cross the road by the pedestrian crossing
  • Walk past the Shaw Foundation Building AS7, until you see AS5
  • Walk towards the AS5 lift and take to level 4, we are at 04-30
  • Walking distance: Approximately 400m
